About Blood Donation

The Donation Process

Blood donation is a simple and impactful way to save lives. Here’s how the process works:

  1. Registration: Provide your personal details and health history.
  2. Eligibility Check: A medical professional will evaluate your health status.
  3. Donation: The actual blood donation takes approximately 10-15 minutes.
  4. Refreshments: Relax and enjoy a snack to replenish your energy.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Age between 18 and 65 years.
  • Weight of at least 60 kg.
  • Good general health and no recent illnesses.
  • No recent tattoos or piercings (within the last 6 months).

Benefits of Blood Donation

  • Helps save lives in emergencies and for patients with chronic conditions.
  • Promotes better health for donors by stimulating new blood cell production.
  • Fosters a sense of community and altruism.

FAQ

1. Is blood donation safe?

Yes, it is completely safe. New and sterile equipment is used for each donor.

2. How often can I donate blood?

Men can donate every 3 months, and women every 4 months.

3. Will I feel weak after donating?

Most donors feel fine after donating. It’s recommended to stay hydrated and avoid strenuous activity for a few hours.
